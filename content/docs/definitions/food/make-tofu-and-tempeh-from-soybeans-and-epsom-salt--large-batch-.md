## Description
 Boil Soybeans, blend, separate okara and ferment with Rhizopus&#10;&#9;&#9;&#9;oligosporus starter. Add vinegar to soymilk as coagulant. This uses&#10;&#9;&#9;&#9;all the okara by-product of tofu.
