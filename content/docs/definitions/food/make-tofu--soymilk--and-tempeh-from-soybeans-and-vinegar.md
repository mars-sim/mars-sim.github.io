## Description
 Boil Soybeans, blend, separate okara and ferment with Rhizopus&#10;&#9;&#9;&#9;oligosporus starter. Add vinegar to some soymilk as coagulant,&#10;&#9;&#9;&#9;sweetening the remainder.
