mars-sim attempts to simulate the overall professional relationship for future settlers on Mars. 

The social aspect of any two individuals is modeled by a 3-tier relationship indicator showing how close or how adversarial between them. 

Each settler bears an imprint of one's gender, date of birth, weight, height, BMI, age, blood type, a personality profile based on Myers-Briggs Type Indicators (MBTI) and Five Factor Model (FFM), and a set of natural attributes (such as Agility, Academic Aptitude, Attractiveness, Conversation, Endurance, Experience Aptitude, Leadership, Strength, Stress Resilience, and Teaching). 

These characteristics set in motion on how well a person interact with his/her own team and others socially and professionally.

How a person relates with others is shown in his/her Relationship Tab. Suffice to say that each settler has unique personality types, coupling with a blend of natural attributes and professional skills, thus creating the destiny of his/her own on Mars.

In addition, a person's Science Tab tracks the ongoing and finished scientific studies in collaboration with others and the scientific achievement in his/her own areas of endeavour.
